About

As an on and off DM over many, many years of playing various versions of Dungeons & Dragons, I've developed a philosophy that a lazy DM is a good DM. Similar to being a programmer, if you can find a tool that automates something there is no reason not to use it. Why spend tons of time creating characters, rolling dice for treasure, and other various tasks that a DM has to do, when you can use tools to automate this process?

Status

For now, I've developed various generators that can be used by DMs and players alike. Soon we'll allow for user generated content: maps, pre-made characters, custom classes, homebrew spells and items, etc. To populate the generators I've gathered information from various D&D books. Right now we only support version 3.5, below you'll find a list of all the books that information was taken from. Thanks from coming and I hope stay a while, spread the word, and give some feedback on how I can make this better!
